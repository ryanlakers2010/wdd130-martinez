Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to explain why music created by human artists is often more powerful and meaningful than music created by artificial intelligence[cite: 3]. Another purpose of this website is to help users understand the value of creativity, emotions, and personal experiences when it comes to creating and writing music[cite: 4]. This website will also discuss the growing use of AI in the music industry and why smaller music creators should still have the opportunity to showcase their work[cite: 5].

Audience

Anyone who loves music and wants to support small creators and artists while learning about the differences between human-created and AI-generated music[cite: 8].

Site Content Outline

1. Home Page

Welcome to Humans vs AI. Music has always been an important way for people to express emotions, tell stories, and connect with others[cite: 22]. Human artists have the ability to use their experiences, creativity, and feelings to create songs that have meaning and purpose, while also connecting with people's hearts[cite: 23]. In recent years, artificial intelligence has become capable of generating music[cite: 24]. This website explores the differences between human-created and AI-generated music and explains why many people believe human creativity remains an important part of music[cite: 25].

2. Topic Page (Important Information)

Humans have been creating music for approximately 40,000 to 50,000 years[cite: 28]. Music has become an essential part of human culture through religious hymns, musical plays, songs, and instrumental performances[cite: 29]. Music has also inspired books, dances, and even movies[cite: 30].

Why has music become such an important part of our lives? One reason is its ability to express personal experiences and emotions[cite: 31, 32]. One example I enjoy is a song by Olivia Rodrigo from her album "GUTS"[cite: 33]. In many of her songs, she shares personal experiences and emotions from her life[cite: 34]. This allows listeners to connect with her music and understand the feelings she is expressing[cite: 35]. That is the power that music can have[cite: 36].

Recently, AI has become capable of producing music through computer programs[cite: 37]. It analyzes music that has already been created and uses patterns to generate new songs[cite: 38]. Even though AI can create music faster than a human, many people believe it lacks one key ingredient: the passion, emotions, and personal experiences that make music meaningful and special[cite: 39].
