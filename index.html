Welcome to Humans VS AI

Music has always been an important way for people to express emotions, tell stories, and connect with others.

Human artists have the ability to use their experiences, creativity, and feelings to create songs that have meaning and purpose while also connecting with people's hearts.

In recent years, artificial intelligence has become capable of generating music. This website explores the differences between human-created and AI-generated music and explains why many people believe human creativity remains an important part of music.

Purpose of This Website

The purpose of this website is to explain why music created by human artists is often more powerful and meaningful than music created by artificial intelligence.

This website also helps users understand the value of creativity, emotions, and personal experiences when creating and writing music.

It will also discuss the growing use of AI in the music industry and why smaller music creators should still have the opportunity to showcase their work.

Who Is This Website For?

This website is for anyone who loves music and wants to support small creators and artists while learning about the differences between human-created and AI-generated music.
